Mercedes Benz

Mercedes-Benz offers a diverse lineup of vehicles organized into several series, each catering to different needs and preferences. Here's an overview:

Sedans and Hatchbacks
A-class Entry-level luxury compact sedan or hatchback.
C-class Compact luxury sedan; a step up in comfort and technology.
E-class Midsize luxury sedan, also available as a coupe or convertible.
S-class Flagship full-size luxury sedan, known for advanced technology and opulence.
CLA Stylish compact sedan with coupe-like design.
CLS Sleek four-door coupe with a sporty profile.


SUVs and Crossovers
GLA Subcompact luxury SUV
GLB Compact SUV with optional three-row seating.
GLC Compact luxury SUV with coupe variant.
GLE Midsize luxury SUV with off-road capabilities; coupe version available.
GLS Full-size luxury SUV with three rows of seating.
G-Class Iconic luxury off-roader with rugged capabilities.
Sports Cars and Performance
AMG GT High-performance sports car with coupe and convertible variants.
SL : Luxury roadster with advanced performance and styling.
Electric Vehicles(EQ Series)
EQA Entry-level electric compact SUV.
EQB Electric compact SUV with optional three-row seating.
EQC Midsize electric SUV.
EQS Sedan Flagship electric luxury sedan.
EQS SUV Electric luxury SUV with spacious design.
EQE Electric midsize sedan.
EQE SUV Electric SUV for urban and long-distance travel.
Other Models:
Maybach Series Ultra-luxury variants of S-Class and GLS for the highest level of luxury.
AMG Variants High-performance versions available across most series, featuring enhanced power, handling, and styling.

BMW

BMW offers a diverse lineup of vehicles categorized into series, catering to various preferences and lifestyles. Here’s an overview:

Sedans, Coupes, and Convertibles:
1 Series Compact hatchback (mostly available outside the U.S.).
2 Series Compact coupe, convertible, and Gran Coupe (sedan-style hatchback).
3 Series Popular compact luxury sedan and wagon (Touring, in select markets).
4 Series Sleek coupe, convertible, and Gran Coupe (four-door coupe style).
5 Series Midsize luxury sedan with a balance of performance and comfort.
7 Series Flagship full-size luxury sedan with cutting-edge technology.
8 Series High-performance luxury coupe, convertible, and Gran Coupe.

SUVs and Crossovers (X Series):
X1 Subcompact luxury SUV.
X2 Sporty subcompact SUV with coupe styling.
X3 Compact luxury SUV with performance options.
X4 Coupe-style version of the X3.
X5 Midsize luxury SUV with advanced features.
X6 Coupe-style version of the X5..
X7 Full-size luxury SUV with three rows of seating.

Electric Vehicles (i Series):
i3 Compact electric hatchback (discontinued in some regions).
i4 Electric Gran Coupe offering sporty dynamics.
i7 Electric version of the 7 Series luxury sedan.
iX Fully electric luxury SUV.
iX3 Electric version of the X3 SUV (available in select markets).

Speciality Models
Z4 Two-seat roadster for open-top driving pleasure.
Alpina Models Exclusive high-performance and luxury-tuned variants of existing BMW models.

Tesla

Tesla offers an innovative lineup of all-electric vehicles designed to combine performance, technology, and sustainability. Here’s a concise overview of their series and models:

Sedans
Model 3 Compact electric sedan with excellent range, performance, and affordability. Available trims: Rear-Wheel Drive, Long Range, Performance.
Model S Luxury electric sedan with advanced features and high performance. Available trims: Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive, Plaid (tri-motor for extreme performance)

SUVs and Crossovers:
Model Y Compact SUV offering spaciousness and versatility Available trims: Long Range, Performance.
Model X Full-size luxury SUV with distinctive falcon-wing doors and high-tech features. Available trims: Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive, Plaid.

Specialty Models:
Cybertruck: Futuristic electric pickup truck with robust capabilities (launch expected soon).
Roadster: High-performance electric sports car (upcoming model designed to set new speed records).

Commercial and Energy Solutions:
Tesla Semi: All-electric heavy-duty truck designed for long-haul efficiency and sustainability (recently launched).
Powerwall, Solar Roof, and Solar Panels: Energy products complementing Tesla’s automotive lineup for home and commercial use.
