Toyota GR Corolla

Price and Specs

The Toyota GR Corolla is a sporty hatchback with a turbocharged engine, all-wheel drive, and a dynamic driving experience.

  • Starting Price: $50,995
  • Performance Enhancements:
    • Increased torque output
    • Weight reduction measures, including removal of rear seats
    • Track-tuned suspension and exclusive performance components
Performance Specifications:
  • Engine: 1.6L turbocharged inline-3
  • Horsepower: 300 hp
  • Torque: 273 lb-ft (Core and Premium); up to 295 lb-ft (Morizo Edition)
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual with rev-matching
  • Drivetrain: GR-FOUR All-Wheel Drive with adjustable torque distribution
  • Fuel Economy: Approximately 21 MPG city / 28 MPG highway
Interior and Features:
  • Seating Capacity: 5 passengers (4 in Morizo Edition due to rear seat removal)
  • Cargo Space: 17.8 cubic feet
  • Infotainment: 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration
  • Safety: Toyota Safety Sense™ suite, including pre-collision system, lane departure alert, and dynamic radar cruise control
